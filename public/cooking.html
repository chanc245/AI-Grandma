焢肉 Kong Rou

Ingredients:
Cubed pork belly: 1 lb
Garlic: around 4 piece
Sugar: 1 Spoon
Green Onion: 2 piece
Soy Sauce: half a bowl
Water: 3 bowl

Steps:
1. Medium Heat, Fry the pork belly to golden brown (skin first), put it aside
2. Medium Heat, Garlic and Green fry to golden brown, put it aside
3. Low Heat, heat sugar til it turns golden, add in the meat, soy sauce, water
4. Low Heat, cook until the water almost all evaporates (almost no water)
